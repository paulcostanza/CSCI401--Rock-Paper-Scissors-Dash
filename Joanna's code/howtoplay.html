Game Manual: Rock Paper Scissors Dash

Welcome to Rock Paper Scissors Dash– a fast-paced arcade game inspired by the classic Pac-Man, with the gameplay of PacMan, for the ultimate playground showdown: Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, and Paper beats Rock. Choose your character and outsmart your opponents before time runs out!

🎮 Laptop Controls

No matter if you're right-handed or left-handed, you're covered:

  • Arrow Keys (↑ ↓ ← →) — Move your character around the map.
  • W A S D Keys — Alternate control option for left-handed players.

📱 Mobile Controls

Virtual Joystick — Classic red joystick from the arcade game.

🌀 Objective

Your mission is simple:

  • Stay alive
  • Avoid enemies that can beat you
  • Collect power boosters
  • Collect pellets
  • Reach exits when available
  • Beat the clock!

🍬 Collectible Pellets

Scattered around each level are collectible pellets:

You must collect all pellets in the level to unlock the next stage.

Pellets add a strategic element — do you go for the last few while enemies close in, or make a break for a booster?

🪨📄✂️ Characters & Combat Rules

Each player takes on the role of one of the iconic rock-paper-scissors characters:

Character Can Defeat Can Be Defeated By
Rock Scissors Paper
Paper Rock Scissors
Scissors Paper Rock

⚔️ Example:

If you're playing as Rock, you can crush Scissors, but you'd better run if Paper shows up!

🏆 Scoring

Your score reflects your performance in each level and is based on:

  • Collecting Pellets — Each pellet adds points to your score.
  • Defeating Enemies — Outsmarting and defeating enemies earns additional points.
  • Bonus Points for Extra Time — The more time left on the clock, the more bonus points you get.

⚡ Boosters

Random levels include glowing Boosters:

  • Collecting a booster gives you a temporary speed boost.
  • Boosts help you escape enemies or reach exits faster.
  • Boost duration is limited – use it wisely!

⏳ Game Over

You lose the game if:

  • You run out of time.
  • You get killed by a stronger character.

So keep moving, keep dodging, and don’t let the timer get the best of you!

🚪 Exits & Teleportation

Some levels feature Exit Portals:

  • Enter one exit, and your character will bounce to another exit elsewhere on the map.
  • Great for escaping enemies or reaching hard-to-get areas.
  • Watch out – enemies can use them too!

🧠 Tips to Win

  • Use boosters to outmaneuver or escape.
  • Stay alert — just like in real rock-paper-scissors, it's all about strategy and timing!
